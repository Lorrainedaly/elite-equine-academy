Where knowledge begins is where success arises. We have worked with riders of all skill levels at the Elite Equine Academy, from elite international competitors to those just starting out in showjumping.


Regardless of where you are in your journey, we will collaborate closely with you to develop a unique personal training programme that will help you to achieve your goals more quickly.

Who we are

As a group of expert showjumpers, we think every rider should have the chance to train with the greatest.


Our goal in opening this academy was to provide a secure, serene, and welcoming space where students and their horses could grow together while receiving a unique personalised training programme.


Our academy is equipped with top of the range facilities.


We have a full size indoor, 2 outdoor sand and fibre arenas, an olympic sized grass arena, a 3/4 mile gallop.


For the liveries on our intensive training courses we have large indoor stables, a solarium room, hot wash bay, and an aqua treadmill.
Ensuring that the horses recieve the best care while they are staying with us.
