A complete individualized programe to give all of our riders the chance to succeed

Success arrises where knowledge begins. Here at the Elite Equine academy we have worked with all levels of riders from the top international riders to riders who are only beginning their showjumping career.

No matter what stage of your journey that you are at, we will work closely with you to develop a unique personal training programme to fast track you to achieving your goals.

Who we are

We are a team of professional showjumpers that believe that every rider should have the opertunity to train with the best.

We set up this academy to create a safe, calm and comfortable environment for students and their horses to train and develop as a team receiving a unique personal training programme.

Our academy is equipped with top of the range facilities.

We have a full size indoor, 2 outdoor sand and fibre arenas, an olympic sized grass arena, a 3/4 mile gallop.

For the liveries on our intensive training courses we have large indoor stables, a solarium room, hot wash bay, and an aqua treadmill.
Ensuring that the horses recieve the best care while they are staying with us.
